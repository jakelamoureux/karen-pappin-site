Definition

What is consultancy?

Consultancy is the practice of providing expert, evidence‑based advice and guidance to help clients solve problems, enhance performance, and navigate change. It focuses on diagnosing needs, co‑creating strategies, and supporting implementation to achieve measurable outcomes.
